Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-17

CryptoBot posts public market updates to X (Twitter). We do not collect personal information from end users. If you authorize CryptoBot to post to your own X account, your access tokens are stored securely by you (e.g., on your server or device) and are not shared with third parties.

Data we process

  • X account identifiers required to publish posts.
  • Technical logs for reliability and abuse prevention (timestamps, error messages). Logs do not include Direct Messages.

What we do not do

  • No advertising profiles, no sale of data.
  • No mass-DMs or auto-follow behavior.

Your rights

To request deletion of stored credentials or logs, contact amachloukh@gmail.com. If tokens are revoked on X, posting stops automatically.
