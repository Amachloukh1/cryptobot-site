CryptoBot – Official App Information

Automated posting of market updates to X (Twitter). Designed for one account, low-frequency posts (1–2 per day), and no DMs or auto-follow.

What this app does

  • Creates public Posts on the connected X account (Read/Write permissions only).
  • No bulk messaging, no auto-follow/unfollow, no scraping.
  • All actions comply with X Automation Rules and Developer Policy.
